# Kova

An AI-powered personal operating system that understands your goals and 
orchestrates your time, focus, and habits around them — proactively, 
not reactively.

## What this is

Most productivity tools work in silos. Calendar apps schedule. 
Focus apps block distractions. Task managers list todos. 
None of them know what you're actually trying to achieve this week.

LifeOS connects the dots. It understands your real objectives, 
reorganizes your schedule around them, and acts before you lose focus — 
not after.

## Current stage

 **Validation** — Interviewing university students about time 
management pain points. Pre-product.

## Hypothesis

Students and knowledge workers lose significant productive time not 
because they lack tools, but because no single system understands 
their goals well enough to act on their behalf.
